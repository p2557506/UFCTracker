Welcome

Intro: This webpage will explain all to know about the Ultimate Fighting Championship. Ranked fighters, upcoming cards and upcoming prospects to look out for.

What is the UFC? An organisation where fighters engage in unarmed combat over a period of either 3 x 5 minute rounds 5 x 5 minute rounds or until the other athlete is unable to contniue. All martial arts are legal.
